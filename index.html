Why Pas?

Also known as 'Pas', the resort is situated at the end of the Grandvalira's 215km skiable slopes. The Grandvalira passes through seven sectors: Encamp, Canillo, El Tarter, Grau Roig, Pas de la Casa, Peretol, and Soldeu. The skiable area is comparable to popular 'trendy' resorts, such as La Plagne (225km), Les 2 Alpes (200km) and Les Arcs (200km).

In addition, the Apres is WILD. With parties starting from mid-afternoon and a beer costing approx €5, you're guaranteed to have a great time without the price leaving a foul taste in your mouth!
